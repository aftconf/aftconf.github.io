Advances in Financial Technologies — AFT 2025

The seventh international conference on Advances in Financial Technologies (AFT’25) is a premier venue for presenting the latest developments in technologies related to novel financial infrastructure such as cryptocurrencies and their applications, blockchains, and exchanges.

The AFT 2025 conference will take place October 7-10, 2025 at Carnegie Mellon University in Pittsburgh, PA, USA.

All accepted research papers will be available as open access.

Affiliated Workshop

The CMU Secure Blockchain Initiative Summit, an invitation-only workshop, will take place on the first day of the conference (October 7, 2025) and the main conference will take place October 8-10, 2025. If you are registered for the main conference, and are interested in attending the CMU Secure Blockchain Initiative Summit, please reach out to Michael Lisanti.
