Advances in Financial Technologies — AFT 2024

The sixth international conference on Advances in Financial Technologies (AFT’24) is a premier venue for presenting the latest developments in technologies related to novel financial infrastructure such as cryptocurrencies and their applications, blockchains, and exchanges.

The AFT 2024 conference will take place September 23-25, 2024 at the Austrian National Bank (OeNB) in Vienna, Austria.

The last day of the conference (Wednesday) will feature sessions that are organized in collaboration with the conference Economics of Payments XIII (EoP) with the goal of fostering an interdisciplinary exchange of views regarding economic and policy aspects of payment, clearing and settlement systems. The day will feature keynotes by Neha Narula, Digital Currency Initiative at MIT, and Charles M. Kahn, University of Illinois and Bank of Canada, as well as a panel discussion, culminating in an evening reception and poster session.
