AFT '24 — Attend

Venue

AFT '24 will be held as an in-person event in Vienna, Austria at the Austrian National Bank (Otto-Wagner-Platz 3, 1090 Vienna) from September 23-25, 2024. Typical map apps work well for navigating Vienna and should suggest convenient routes to the Austrian National Bank. Please note that there will be no remote attendance or streaming available for this event.

Each paper accepted to the conference needs to be accompanied with distinct author registration for the corresponding day in which the paper is to be presented. A paper presented during Day 1 to Day 2 is expected to register for the Main Conference (3-Day). Work presented at the workshops is expected to register for the Workshops (Thursday). Authors must register by the deadline of Sept 2nd, 2024. Papers without a valid accompanying registration may be excluded from the proceedings.

Traveling to Vienna

By Plane

Vienna, centrally located in Europe, is easily accessible from around the world through Vienna International Airport (VIE). The airport provides direct connections to major European cities and hubs, as well as to significant international destinations. The rapid transit railroad (S-Bahn) offers a cost-effective way to commute between the city of Vienna and Vienna Airport within 30 minutes. Taxis are also readily available.

By Train

Vienna offers excellent train connections to major European cities, including Zurich (8 hours), Munich (4 hours), Prague (4 hours), Budapest (3 hours), and Frankfurt (6 hours). The Austrian Federal Railways, known as ÖBB, is the national railway company of Austria and provides further information on schedules and fares.

Public Transport

Vienna has a well-developed public transport network that is affordable, frequent, fast, clean, efficient, and rarely overcrowded. Wiener Linien is the municipal transport authority, and their website provides further information on schedules and available tickets.

Student Support

We have limited funding to potentially support attendance of a few participants with financial needs. This is generally intended for presenters who can secure some but not all of their own funding, although we will consider applications from all participants.

Eligibility: Applicants to this grant must be a student at the time of physically attending AFT '24 to qualify. Generally, travel grants are awarded to PhD students pursuing research in areas of interest to AFT. We rarely consider master's students and do not consider postdocs.

Your application must include the following:

  • A resume with complete contact information and correct email address.
  • A short personal statement (up to 250 words) detailing the relevance of the conference to your research and career development, a breakdown of estimated expenses (conference registration, travel, and lodging), and an explanation of why you cannot attend AFT without a travel grant.
  • A letter (maximum 1 page) from your advisor (or faculty contact) confirming your good standing at the institution, explaining the benefits and potential contributions of your attendance at AFT, describing your current funding status and need for the travel grant, and committing to supplement the travel award to cover remaining costs.

Please submit your application by Sept. 1, 2024, via email to aft2024.chairs@gmail.com including your CV and Reference Letter as a single PDF.
