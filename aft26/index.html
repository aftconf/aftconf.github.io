Advances in Financial Technologies — AFT 2026

The eighth international conference on Advances in Financial Technologies (AFT’26) is a premier venue for presenting the latest developments in technologies related to novel financial infrastructure such as cryptocurrencies and their applications, blockchains, and exchanges.

The AFT 2026 conference will take place on October 6-9, 2026 at London School of Economics in London, UK.
