Advances in Financial Technologies (AFT)

Welcome

The international conference on Advances in Financial Technologies aims to be a premier venue presenting the latest developments in technologies related directly and indirectly to novel financial infrastructure such as cryptocurrencies and their applications, blockchains, and exchanges.
